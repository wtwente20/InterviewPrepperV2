Getting Started

Welcome to your interview preparation journey! To get the most out of our application, here's a quick guide:

  • Interview Questions: Start by exploring and answering the default interview questions. Feel free to add any new questions you encounter.
  • Interview Schedule: Keep track of your upcoming interviews with our easy-to-use calendar.
  • Performance Reviews: After each interview, rate your performance and review past assessments to improve.
  • Interview Resources: Access and save valuable resources, such as articles, books, and videos, to aid your preparation.
  • Account Settings: Manage your personal details and account settings with ease.
